The Story

Dinner, staged like a performance.

Khana Drama brings Jamshedpur its first taste of molecular gastronomy — familiar North Indian, Mughlai and Chinese favorites, replated with foam, smoke and a little theatre. Every dish is built to be tasted twice: once with the eyes, once with the fork.

Dim lighting, low music and a kitchen that treats plating as choreography make it a favorite for anniversaries, quiet dates and long dinners with people you actually want to talk to.
