The Story

Dinner, staged like a performance.

Khana Drama brings Jamshedpur its first taste of molecular gastronomy — familiar North Indian, Mughlai and Chinese favourites, replated with foam, smoke and a touch of theatre. Every dish is built to be tasted twice: once with the eyes, once with the fork.

Dim lighting, low music and a kitchen that treats plating as choreography make it a favourite for anniversaries, quiet dates, family celebrations and long dinners with people you actually want to talk to.
